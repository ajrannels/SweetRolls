    ABOUT US    

Sweet Rolls began in the once small Roundtown, North Carolina back in 1943. It was created by the Butternut family who loved to serve the community with delicious sweet donuts of both regular and unusual flavors invented by them. It is still owned by the same family passed down a few generations in the now booming Roundtown. The same donut recipes are used as long as some new creative ones. Come on down to the Sweet Rolls family and enjoy delicious donut treats!
